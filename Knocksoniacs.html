KNOCKSONIACS

"Knocksoniacs", derived from two words Knock and Sonic Boom, was founded in 2014 by Deepak Lamba under SAE Colligative Club-IIT Bhubaneswar. Team Knocksoniacs, with the goal of performing well in the SAE competitions, has a holistic vision. We aim towards learning, application, innovation, and teaching. We aim towards providing the students with the knowledge of automotive engineering, giving a practical experience- something that most of the colleges in India fail to do, and also build an atmosphere where the young minds work together to innovate, and potentially revolutionize the automotive industry.
